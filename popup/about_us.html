Our Tool
Our tool aims to make privacy policies more accessible and comprehensible for users. By analyzing the text of privacy policies on websites, our extension provides clear summaries and key insights, helping users make informed decisions about their online privacy. This tool will streamline the process of understanding complex legal language and highlight important privacy practices, ultimately fostering greater transparency in the digital world.

Academic Foundation
Our project is grounded in research and methodologies provided by our professor at Politecnico di Bari. We have extensively studied the sources and papers recommended, which have guided our approach to developing a robust and effective tool. This academic foundation ensures that our solution is both innovative and grounded in scholarly research.
